# Help Desk Ticket Worksheet

## 1. What Is It?

A help desk ticket is a record of a technology issue, request, or service need.

## 2. Why Does It Matter?

Tickets matter because they document work, track support needs, and reveal recurring problems.

## 3. Where It Shows Up At Work

A good ticket captures who is affected, what happened, when it happened, what was tried, and how it was resolved.

## 4. How It Connects To AI

AI can help summarize tickets, suggest knowledge articles, identify trends, and support routing, but ticket data must be accurate.

## 5. How It Connects To Cybersecurity

Tickets may reveal security incidents, suspicious access, phishing reports, or recurring control failures.

## 6. How It Connects To Infrastructure

Infrastructure teams use ticket trends to identify outages, system issues, capacity concerns, and service degradation.

## 7. How It Connects To Business

Ticket data helps leaders understand demand, staffing, user pain points, root causes, and automation opportunities.

## 8. Interview-Ready Answer

A help desk ticket documents a user issue or request so IT can track, prioritize, resolve, and report on the work.

## 9. Leadership-Ready Answer

Ticket data helps leaders understand demand, root causes, service quality, staffing needs, and opportunities for automation or process improvement.
