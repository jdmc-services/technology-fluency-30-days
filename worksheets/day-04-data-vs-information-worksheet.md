# Data vs. Information Worksheet

## 1. What Is It?

Data is raw facts. Information is data that has been organized, interpreted, or given context so it becomes meaningful.

## 2. Why Does It Matter?

This matters because technology decisions depend on turning raw data into useful, trusted information.

## 3. Where It Shows Up At Work

Ticket counts, login attempts, system alerts, call volumes, and usage reports become valuable only when interpreted correctly.

## 4. How It Connects To AI

AI depends on data, but useful AI outcomes require context, quality, and interpretation.

## 5. How It Connects To Cybersecurity

Security teams turn raw events into information that can identify risk, attacks, or control gaps.

## 6. How It Connects To Infrastructure

Infrastructure teams use data from monitoring systems to understand performance, capacity, and outages.

## 7. How It Connects To Business

Leaders need information, not just raw numbers, to make decisions.

## 8. Interview-Ready Answer

Data is raw facts. Information is data that has been processed or placed in context so it can support understanding or decisions.

## 9. Leadership-Ready Answer

Organizations create value when raw data becomes trustworthy information that supports decisions, operations, risk management, and strategy.

## 10. Practice Exercise

Find one example of raw data and explain what information it provides.

## 11. Reflection Question

Can the same data lead two people to different conclusions? Why?
