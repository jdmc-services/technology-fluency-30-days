# AI Hallucination Worksheet

## 1. What Is It?

An AI hallucination happens when an AI system gives an answer that sounds confident but is wrong, unsupported, or made up.

## 2. Why Does It Matter?

AI hallucination matters because polished output can still be inaccurate and may influence decisions, documents, or workflows.

## 3. Where It Shows Up At Work

AI-generated summaries, emails, policies, technical instructions, clinical content, legal content, and reports need human review.

## 4. How It Connects To AI

Hallucination is a core AI literacy issue. Users must understand that AI output is not automatically truth.

## 5. How It Connects To Cybersecurity

Wrong AI output can affect confidentiality, integrity, and availability if it leads to poor decisions or unsafe actions.

## 6. How It Connects To Infrastructure

Bad AI-generated technical guidance can cause misconfiguration, downtime, or incorrect troubleshooting.

## 7. How It Connects To Business

Executives and teams need verification steps before AI output influences customers, patients, operations, or decisions.

## 8. Interview-Ready Answer

An AI hallucination is when a model produces incorrect or unsupported information while presenting it as accurate.

## 9. Leadership-Ready Answer

AI outputs should be verified before they influence decisions, documentation, customers, patients, operations, or system changes.

## 10. Practice Exercise

Ask AI a technical question. Verify the answer using at least one trusted source.

## 11. Reflection Question

What kind of AI answer would you never trust without checking?
