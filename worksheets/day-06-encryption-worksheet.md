# Encryption Worksheet

## 1. What Is It?

Encryption converts readable data into unreadable data so only authorized users or systems with the right key can access it.

## 2. Why Does It Matter?

Encryption matters because it protects sensitive information while stored or transmitted.

## 3. Where It Shows Up At Work

Encryption protects online banking, messaging, cloud storage, healthcare data, business records, and authentication flows.

## 4. How It Connects To AI

AI platforms may process sensitive prompts, documents, embeddings, logs, or outputs that require encryption in transit and at rest.

## 5. How It Connects To Cybersecurity

Encryption supports confidentiality and reduces the impact of data exposure.

## 6. How It Connects To Infrastructure

Infrastructure teams manage certificates, TLS, encrypted storage, VPNs, secure communications, and key management.

## 7. How It Connects To Business

Encryption helps build trust, protect data, support compliance, and reduce exposure risk.

## 8. Interview-Ready Answer

Encryption protects data by converting it into an unreadable format that can only be restored with the proper key.

## 9. Leadership-Ready Answer

Encryption is a foundational trust control that helps protect sensitive data, reduce exposure, and support security and compliance requirements.
