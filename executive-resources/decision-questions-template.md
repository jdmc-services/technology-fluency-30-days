# Executive Decision Questions Template

Use this template to help leaders ask better questions about a technology concept.

## Concept

[Technology concept]

## Strategic Questions

1. Why does this matter to the organization?
2. What business process depends on it?
3. What problem does it solve?
4. What risk does it reduce?
5. What opportunity does it create?

## Ownership Questions

1. Who owns this capability?
2. Who supports it daily?
3. Who approves changes?
4. Who monitors performance?
5. Who is accountable if it fails?

## Security and Governance Questions

1. What controls are in place?
2. What policies apply?
3. How is access managed?
4. How is activity logged?
5. How often is it reviewed?

## Operational Questions

1. How is it monitored?
2. What happens if it fails?
3. What is the escalation path?
4. What documentation exists?
5. What training is needed?

## Financial Questions

1. What does it cost?
2. How is usage tracked?
3. Where could waste occur?
4. What is the cost of failure?
5. What investment is needed?

## AI-Era Questions

1. Does this concept affect AI tools or workflows?
2. Does AI depend on this data, system, or access path?
3. Could AI amplify the risk?
4. Is human review required?
5. What governance is needed?

## Final Decision Question

What decision should be made now, and what information is still missing?
