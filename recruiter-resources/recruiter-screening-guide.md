## Applied Screening Question

## Weak Answer Signals

A weak answer may:

- Only repeat a memorized definition
- Use jargon without explanation
- Fail to connect the concept to work
- Show no troubleshooting awareness
- Show no security or risk awareness

## Strong Answer Signals

A strong answer usually includes:

- A simple explanation
- A real-world example
- Awareness of users or business impact
- Basic troubleshooting logic
- Connection to security, infrastructure, cloud, data, or AI

## Notes For Recruiters

A candidate does not need to be an expert to show potential.

Look for clarity, curiosity, structured thinking, and the ability to explain what they know.
